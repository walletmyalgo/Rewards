What Makes Algorand a Unique Blockchain

The five key features of the Algorand blockchain are Pure-Proof-of-Stake, Global Scale, Broad Decentralization, Robust Security, and Guaranteed Finality. The Algorand blockchain delivers on the key promise of blockchain technology, removing the pain and cost created by bad processes, intermediaries, middlemen, fees, and artificial delays in today's economy.

Decentralizing Algorand Governance

The Algorand Foundation believes in a fully decentralized Algorand ecosystem. Such a decentralized ecosystem, in which everyone can impact monetary, technical, and other policy decisions, best enables the Algorand community to shape the shared future of the Algorand network. We are pleased to announce the launch of Decentralizing Algorand Governance, the proposal for the long term governance of the Algorand ecosystem.
